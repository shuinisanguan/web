Landscape

Chongqing’s tourism with a long history of three thousand years is rich in resources. It has magnificent natural scenery integrating mountains, water, forests, springs, waterfalls, gorges, caves, etc. It also has Rong Bayu culture, ethnic culture, immigration culture, Three Gorges culture, It is a rich cultural landscape that combines capital culture and urban culture.

Delicacy

The biggest feature of Chongqing cuisine is spicy. The focus is on hemp, which is prickly ash. Even if you don’t put the chili peppers, you can still feel spicy. Generally speaking, the taste is relatively heavy (heavy oil, heavy spicy).

As one of the seventeen ancient city gates in Chongqing before, it is the hub of Jialing River and Yangtze River and the largest waterway passenger terminal in Chongqing.

The Monument to the Victory of the Anti-Japanese War and the People's Liberation Monument, also known as the Monument to the Victory of the Anti-Japanese War, the Monument to the People's Liberation, and the "Liberation Monument" for short.
