The evolution of Chongqing

In the Paleolithic age of two million years ago, people lived in Chongqing, and Chongqing has a civilization history of more than 3000 years.

Stone Age

In the early Paleolithic period 2 million years ago, the earliest human beings in China, Wushan people, appeared in Wushan County, Chongqing City. In the late Paleolithic period between 20,000 and 30,000 years ago, the "Tongliang Culture" appeared.

The reign of successive dynasties

Chongqing was the main city of the dynasties, and it was also a battleground for military strategists. The evolution of thousands of years has produced a unique Bashu culture.

The period of municipality directly under the central government

Chongqing has been directly administered three times, making it a very major city in China. It is also a very promising city.

The Chongqing bombing

The Chongqing bombing meant that during the Anti-Japanese War, Chongqing became China's wartime companion capital and the political, economic, military, and cultural center of the rear. In order to achieve its evil purpose of destroying the large rear base of the Chinese War of Resistance and forcing the Chongqing National Government to surrender, the Japanese fascists carried out long-term and indiscriminate bombings on Chongqing and its surrounding areas, known as the "Chongqing Bombing" in history.
