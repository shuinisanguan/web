Travel to Chongqing

Tourist attractions that Chongqing can't miss.

磁器口

Ciqikou Ancient Town in Chongqing is located on the Bank of Jialing River in Shapingba District, Chongqing. It is a national AAAA scenic spot, a famous historical and cultural street in China, and a "new Bayu twelve sceneries" in Chongqing. Ciqikou Ancient Town in Chongqing was built in Song Dynasty. It has a unique landform of "one river, two streams, three mountains and four streets", forming a natural harbor. It is an important land and water wharf beside Jialing River. Its scenic spots include zhongjiayuan, Xinji grocery store, etc., which was once praised as "little Chongqing".

洪崖洞

Hongya cave is the most popular scenic spot in Chongqing. Because of its unique architectural appearance and spectacular night scenery, it has become the first scenic spot in Chongqing. Night is the most beautiful time for Hongya cave. Not only are the 11 storey huge buildings lit up by bright lights, but also it is the best place to look for Chongqing food and nightlife bars.

鹅岭公园

Eling, formerly known as E-Xiang Ling, is located on the steep and narrow mountains in the north of the Yangtze River and Jialing River. It looks like a goose neck, so it gets its name. The two rivers surrounding Chongqing Peninsula are the closest to each other. It is said that Li Yan, the governor of the Shuhan Dynasty, once wanted to cut through the high cliff to connect the two rivers. With its back against the mountain city, eling was born tall and looked to the West with two rivers, enjoying the majestic, dangerous, broad and beautiful natural scenery.

四川美术学院

Compared with other popular scenic spots in Chongqing, Chuanmei campus is full of trees and quiet. You can't miss the art academy, the art gallery and the tank depot. Graffiti outside the school, a street full of building walls are all kinds of graffiti, become a net red punch. There are a lot of snacks around, including tikan Douhua and Huji Tihua soup, which are cheap and worth a try.
